About me

I graduated from CU Boulder in January 2022 with a bachelors degree in Journalism and a minor in creative technology and design. I am passionate about visual arts and design, as well as storytelling through compelling and truthful journalism.

I have experience in adobe programs including illustrator, photoshop, indesign, after effects, and premiere. My collaborative news article on psychedelic psychotherapy was published in the CU Bold in 2021.

In my free time I enjoy running with my dog, playing guitar, reading, and snowboarding.
